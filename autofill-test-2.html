Contact Autofill Test

Use this page to test Safari's Contacts autofill. Before testing:

  1. Ensure Safari Preferences → AutoFill → Using info from my contacts is enabled.
  2. Have a contact saved in macOS/iOS Contacts with full name, email, phone, and address.
  3. Open this page over HTTPS (recommended). Click into a field and choose the autofill suggestion.

Tip: click an input (e.g., "Given name" or "Email") to trigger Safari's autofill suggestion, then choose a contact to fill the form.
